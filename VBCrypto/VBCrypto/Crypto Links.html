Cryptography Links
Compiled by: David Midkiff

  • Introduction to Cryptography - An excellent source of basic information by Tatu Ylönen (author of Secure Shell).
  • Encryption and Security Tutorial - A good cryptography tutorial.
  • Handbook of Applied Crytography - Online version of this popular and well structured book.
  • Applied Cryptography - Schneier's world famous powerhouse book of Cryptography.
  • PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) - The homepage of the famous PGP software package.
  • RSA Data Security, Inc. - The brand name of Cryptography.
  • Crypto Law Survey - A survey of cryptography laws in more then 30 countries.
  • SSH (Secure Shell) - Homepage of the powerful security communications software package.
  • Cryptographic Reference Center
  • SRP (Secure Remote Password) - Homepage of a cryptographically secure remote access suite.
  • The Shmoo Group - Excellent crypto archive with information on cracking DES.
  • Speak Freely - Secure voice communication software.
  • North American Cryptography Archives
  • Packetstorm Cryptography Archive
  • CanCrypt - Canadian cryptography resources.
  • 11th Alliance - Crypto related tools/downloads.
  • IBM Zurich Security and Cryptography Sources
  • NIST - Government standards and algorithms.
  • Adam's Crypto Resources - Good page full of crypto related articles.
  • Cypherspace.org
  • The Bunker - Cryptography archive stored in a ex-military nuclear bunker. How reassuring ... :)
  • Free non-American Crypto Sites
  • Quadralay Cryptography Archive
  • Ron Rivest Links - The author of the MD5 algorithm and many others provides a shitload of links.
  • Crypto++ - Free cryptography schemes archive for C++.
  • Nautilus Secure Phone Homepage
  • Counterpane Labs Crypto Links - Schneier's crypto link archive.
  • Neil Johnson's Crypto Sources - Good archive of crypto related sources.
  • Ciphers by Ritter - Genius cryptographer and his vast archive of articles and files.
  • Cryptography Research - Tons of crypto resources.
  • The Argon - Security and crypto site.
  • AES - NIST's Advanced Encryption Standard Homepage
  • GILC Crypto Policy Page - Excellent resources on cryptography export/policy.
  • EFF - Privacy, Crypto, ITAR, AER and Encryption Export archive.
  • The International PGP Homepage
  • NSA - The United States government's crypto backbone. Has some good resources on their site.
  • Linux Security - Vast resources for Linux related security.
  • Cypherpunks - Excellent archive of links, rants and downloads.
  • PSCode's Visual Basic Crypto Archive - A lot of cheap homemade ciphers from kids but also quite a few top notch algorithms ported from C to VB. Good source of cryptography for VB programmers.
  • Cryptography for Visual Basic - Homepage of an excellent book on CryptoAPI.
  • Matt Blaze's Cryptography Resource
  • DriveCrypt - Secure hard drive encryption (formerly Scramdisk).
